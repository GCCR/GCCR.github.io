Patient Organizations


Have you lost your sense of smell and taste? Smell and taste loss can make you feel anxious, not interested in food, isolated, and depressed. If this has happened to you, below are some resources for you to visit.

Altered Eating

Altered Eating Research Network: Thought for food.| Rediscovering the joy of food | Research with patients and the public | Faculty of Medical Sciences, Newcastle University, UK

Visit site
Fifth Sense

Fifth Sense is the charity for people affected by smell and taste disorders. We provide education, support, advice and signposting to potential treatment available to people affected by these conditions.

Visit site
AbScent

The home of NoseWell, the Covid-19 smell loss guide. AbScent is the largest online support community providing trusted information and practical resources for everyone living with smell disorders. A charity registered in England and Wales number 1183468

STANA - The Smell and Taste Association of North America.

"To smell the roses and taste the spice of life." We are a patient advocacy group formed in 2020 to improve the quality-of-life for people with smell and taste disorders since we recognize that the loss of taste or smell can affect health, safety and emotional well-being. We seek to expand research, education and treatments for people living in North America who are experiencing the invisible loss of these senses.
